We should own our online identities.
Ember commits to put the users in control of the way they interact with it.
We know a few things we should avoid, and some ideas we should embrace, but we commit to get better at it together.

Please choose a username, and a password.
As you see, we do not ask for an email address or phone number.
We will confirm our identities through real interactions in real life.
There will be no benefit in creating multiple accounts, most interactions with Ember would require a confirmed real identity anyway.
Before your identity is confirmed, you will not be able to recover your account if you forget your password.
